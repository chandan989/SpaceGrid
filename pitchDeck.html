Space Grid shatters the barrier between digital ledgers and physical space. Using a mobile device's GPS, operators emit a "Quantum Trail" to cryptographically capture geographic polygons.

1. Trail Emission (DePIN)

Physical movement generates continuous coordinates broadcast via WebSocket.

2. Territory Capture (RWA)

Closing a loop calculates the enclosed area via PostGIS.

3. Interference Warfare

Intersecting an active, unclosed trail of a rival collapses their loop potential.

4. Grid Economics

Stake CTC to enter high-value sectors and earn passive network dividends.
